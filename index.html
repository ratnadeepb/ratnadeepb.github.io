I am Ratnadeep Bhattacharya, a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science, George Washington University. I am part of the Cloud Systems Lab and my advisor is Prof. Timothy Wood. I have 12 years of experience in the industry designing and building data centers

My area of research is distributed systems with a focus on distributed load balancing and autoscaling for microservices networks. I am also exploring the role of machine learning and related methods in infrastructure engineering.
